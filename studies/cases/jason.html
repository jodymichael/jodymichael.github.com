Case Studies :: Small Business Coaching

Jason: An About Face

Situation:
Jason founded a firm that provided hardware and software solutions for the XYZ industry. He had practiced business skills and the pedigree of an MBA from a prestigious school. Jason's long-term intentions were to build this company's value, sell it in five to ten years, and retire comfortably on the proceeds of the sale.

However, the firm opened its doors just prior to 9/11. Due to the subsequent economic fallout, his business experienced more turbulence in its formative months than it would have under normal conditions. Jason became very concerned.

He had to provide confident leadership at a time when he was well aware that the company was in serious trouble. He feared losing his employees, if they sensed the company situation, so he chose to shoulder this tremendous burden alone and figure out solutions without counsel. With his company struggling to stay afloat, his energy and confidence was going down with the company's bleak outlook. Alone, desperate, and investing what he believed might very well be his "last dollars" he called Jody Michael & Associates for some targeted business advice.

Resolution:
Jody Michael provided Jason a savvy confidante and advocate. Their contract allowed him a confidential sounding wall and a safe place to regroup. Stripped of the politics and complexities of the workplace, coach and client communicated efficiently to achieve amazing results in a very short time.

In an extended first session, Jody Michael worked with Jason in two areas in which she felt were pivotal for Jason's comeback: (1) immediately restore his confidence and enthusiasm and (2) redirect his selling/marketing to more effectively close sells in the XYZ industry. From a state of confusion, anxiety and uncertainty, Jason left that first session hopeful, excited and challenged. Already a driven businessman, he now had recaptured his confident demeanor, redirected his focus, and was ready to implement the strategies that JMA had suggested.

Outcome:
In eight years of coaching, this is the business turnaround Jody marvels at most. After one face-to-face session, Jason accomplished a 180-degree turn-around psychologically and sustained it throughout the week. He was on fire. Jason now had the psychological foundation necessary to revive the firm. His enthusiasm was infectious, his team was inspired, and his actions were focused. He began to make tough decisions and take bold actions.

Due to his tremendous flexibility, decisiveness and focus, Jason achieved stability and growth in his business and took employee morale to new heights. His business was going to not only survive, but thrive.

After the initial crisis passed, Jason continued to work with Jody Michael & Associates. The coaching changed and evolved as the company did. Later coaching topics included an overhaul of the firm's marketing strategy, management of personnel issues, and sales force performance improvement. With his business humming along, Jason will be the first to tell you, those "last dollars" were some of the best he ever spent.
