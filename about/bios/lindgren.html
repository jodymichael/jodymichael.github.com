Laura Lindgren

Entering real estate sales was a natural progression for Laura Lindgren. She managed apartment buildings for 15 years and fully enjoyed the process of helping people. She was a natural fit for a 225 unit high-rise condominium building in River North selling to first time buyers, empty nesters, and investors.

"Laura is an extremely diligent and hard working person. Her follow through and attention to detail are phenomenal," says Millie Rosenbloom, CEO of The Habitat Company. "If you want something done - and done right the first time, call Laura," Millie added.

A graduate of Harrington Institute of Design, Laura now uses her discriminating eye in design to help home owners stage and sell their homes at the most competitive price possible. Laura currently resides in the Lakeview neighborhood with her husband, teenage daughter and two adorable pugs named Otis and Twyla.
