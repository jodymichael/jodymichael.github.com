Jody Michael, Principal

Jody Michael, M.A., L.C.S.W., is founder and president of Jody Michael & Associates. She is a career, executive, and small business coach who helps individuals and businesses reach their goals and achieve their dreams. Jody brings to her coaching practice over 15 years of corporate leadership in the finance industry, entrepreneurial experience, teaching credentials, and over eight years of experience in coaching and psychotherapy. Prior to founding Jody Michael & Associates, she held senior management roles as a commodities trader and broker. She managed over 100 employees on three trading floors, and played key roles in the hiring, the cultural acclimation, and the management, leadership, growth and development of revenue-producing divisions. She worked at prestigious firms such as Goldman Sachs, Kidder-Peabody, and CRT/Nations Bank.

Jody's formal education includes an M.A. in Social Service Administration at the University of Chicago, where she was also awarded a full scholarship and stipend to pursue a Ph.D. She also has a B.S. in Education/Kinesiology from Southern Illinois University, coaching coursework at Coach University and has completed a three-year Ontological Business Design course studying under mentor Fernando Flores and Julio Olalla (renowned educator of coaches).

Jody's professional affiliations include membership in the International Coaching Federation (ICF) and the National Association of Social Workers (NASW). She also has classroom teaching experience at the University of Chicago and has held seminars at Kellogg/Northwestern University.
