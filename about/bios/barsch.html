Jeff Barsch & JB Graphics

JB Graphics [JBG] provides custom printing and direct mail fulfillment to businesses both large and small. Though incorporated in 1999, the firm serves its clients with the sophistication of a seasoned pro and the service of a family friend. Together, the father and son duo that founded the company account for over 35 years of experience in the industry. They possess hands-on familiarity with nearly all facets of print production, customer service, and communications strategy.

This background places JBG at a unique position in the print industry. Traditionally, customers have had to choose between large production houses that offer volume, a range of equipment, but marginal service, or smaller vendors who favor personal service, but are frequently limited by a lack of equipment and the extent of their know-how.

Inspired by this service vacuum, Jeff and Bob Barsch opened JB Graphics to serve those clients who require personal service, expert advice and a multitude of printing options. Today, Jeff and his knowledgeable staff offer a wide range of production capabilities and the patient guidance that guarantees all projects receive the attention and specific equipment that will compliment the messages they carry.

In addition to print production, JB Graphics offers clients turn-key direct marketing solutions. This service provides all of the capabilities a business needs to build its brand equity via direct mail or print media. Clients employ JBG’s marketing strategy consultation, design and copywriting services in the development of many of their campaigns. Rather than bear the personnel expense, loss of productivity, and occasional costly missteps, many businesses prefer to hand-off every task associated with direct mail production. JBG clients utilize our professionals to coordinate list rental, printing, mailing, storage and other execution details. In short, JBG takes print campaigns from idea to result quickly and efficiently.
