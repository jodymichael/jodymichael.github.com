Neena Vlamis

Neena Vlamis, President and Co-founder of A & N Mortgage Services, enjoys a challenge. In particular, she and her thirty-four colleagues specialize in finding the best mortgage product for each of their home-buying customers. Whereas many financial institutions shy away from customers who have less-than-perfect credit records, Neena and A & N boast a winning record with clients from all ends of the spectrum--good credit, bad credit, or no credit.

While Neena and the professionals at A & N assist all clients to find the best mortgage product for their needs, they demonstrate an incredible range of talent when the client-situation requires creative solutions. A & N succeeds in difficult home-financing scenarios as a result of its unparalleled commitment to providing honest advice, its extensive breadth of personal finance knowledge vis-à-vis home ownership, and the strength of its relationships with over fifty banks. These three assets, in combination, have built A & N Mortgage Services into the premier originator of residential home loans in the Chicagoland area.

Whether you enjoy a great credit history or have suffered a few mistakes in the past, you can be confident that Neena Vlamis and A & N Mortgage will identify the optimal financial product and strategy for your specific home-ownership need.
