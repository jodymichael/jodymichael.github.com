Tristan Tamplin & Verso Design

Verso believes that even the smallest design details affect everyday perception and thought. Their work spans both traditional and more modern media, from illustration and photography, to website design and programming. They specialize in logo creation and branding, packaging and periodical design, and highly-customized website development.

Tristan Tamplin – He has been designing and building websites for a broad spectrum of clients since 1998. Proficient in a wide range of multimedia applications and internet technologies, he can help you define and hand build a website specifically crafted to suit your taste and needs. Tristan is also an experienced product, portrait, and fine art photographer.

Dennis Pryber – Dennis graduated in 1992 with a BFA in advertising from the Columbus College of Art & Design. He has a versatile and innovative style with a keen ability to understand the clients’ vision. His over 10 years of design experience ranges from identity, advertising/promotion, magazine editorial, textbook, children’s book, product catalog, and packaging. Dennis is also experienced in illustration and product photography.

Brad Johnson – Brad graduated from Missouri State with a BFA in graphic design and illustration in 1998. His design experience spans the design industry, from logos to web, to product positioning and advertising. Though trained as a classical print designer, Brad has spent the last few years as an art director for a fortune 500 company, focusing mainly on advertising, and identity recognition. Through this, he has also become skilled at market placement and a strong technical illustrator.
