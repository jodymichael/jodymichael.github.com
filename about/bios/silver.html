Dina Silver

Dina Silver offers her advertising and marketing expertise to our small business clients. While she boasts experience in both B2B and B2C industries, she cut her teeth in the retail environment. With more than ten years in the business, Silver counts Sears, Target, The Illinois Lottery, The Limited, and Bigsby & Kruthers Clothiers in the list of brands she has helped build.

A freelance marketing specialist, Silver furnishes both copywriting and creative direction to her clients. She credits her success to a balance between imagination and adherence to marketing principles. Her executions have included print, radio, sales promotions, direct mail, catalog, web, television, and POS media. She is a graduate of Purdue University with a BA in Journalism and Mass Communications.
