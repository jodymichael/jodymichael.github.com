Lisa Mann

Lisa is a decisive marketer who can create order out of chaos by clearly defining objectives and maintaining focus on results. She has a proven track record of results with both entrepreneurial ventures and established businesses of all sizes. Lisa is particularly skilled in creating and implementing new business propositions, strategic plans, market research and brand building initiatives.

Lisa is a founding partner of The Marketing Studio, a marketing consulting firm whose mission is to develop growth strategies and new product concepts that will deliver profitable top line business results for its clients. She has led projects for large companies such as BBDO Atlanta, Pillsbury’s Green Giant brand, Keebler’s Murray Biscuit Company, Caribou Coffee and for entrepreneurs working to introduce new businesses in the food, toy and game, and cable industries. Lisa led the BBDO Atlanta new business team in landing the Simmons Mattress account, $10 million in billings, during a three agency competitive bid. While working with a local entrepreneur, Lisa managed a regional test market of a niche gourmet ketchup, Grand Lyon Bordeaux Ketchup. She was responsible for creating and implementing a fully integrated marketing plan including advertising, consumer promotion, and public relations events. Ultimately Lisa worked on the sale of this business to a Fortune 500 company.

Prior to her consulting career Lisa held executive positions with Kraft Foods and The Hewlett-Packard Company. She began her marketing career with Kraft Foods where she was instrumental in establishing the Beverage Division as a leader in the juice drink category. Earlier, as a sales manager with Hewlett-Packard, Lisa honed her selling skills by achieving sales growth in her geographic territory despite a high tech recession. Her accomplishments have earned her a Georgia Marketing Max Award and a place in Who’s Who of American Business Women.

Lisa holds a Masters in Business Administration from The Harvard Business School. She has a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering, awarded cum laude, from Tufts University.
