Software Options

Due to Jody Michael's tenure in trading and financial services, many clients hail from these industries. In an effort to address their needs, JMA partners with Software Options.

Software Options is an independent firm that develops transaction processing systems for the trading of derivative financial products. The firm caters to individuals and small trading companies that require back-office exchange communication tools and the flexibility that can only be found in a customized package. Clients increase profit and efficiency, limit risk, manage communications according to their own parameters, and retain their ability to modify proprietary algorithms and trading styles in-house.

The elegance and dependability of Software Options products are a result of the two principals' 30+ years of experience designing, building, and implementing systems in the trading industry.

Cathy Cullen, Principal – Software Options: Cathy Cullen, the founder of Software Options, has been a technologist in this field since 1987. She was a lead developer for the first electronic trading system implemented worldwide, Project A at the Chicago Board of Trade. Cullen has specialized knowledge in the technologies that drive risk management for OTC derivative products.

Ben Galewsky, Principal – Software Options: Before his work at Software Options, Galewski was the Chief Technical Architect for Swiss Bank's investment banking division, UBC Warburg. In this position, he developed the division's business process architecture and led development of an interest rate derivative trading system. He provides seventeen years experience as a technologist in the trading industry, as well.
